Guess My Number!

?

This game generates a random number between 1 and 20. The player has 20 attempts to guess the number correctly. If the guess is too high, the messager "Too high!" will be displayed on the right side of the screen. If the guess is too low, the message "too low!" will be displayed instead. Once the number is guessed correctly your highscore will be saved for this current session. Hit "Reset!" to play again and try for a new highscore. Use the X or Press Esc to exit this window.
